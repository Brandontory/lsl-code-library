Instructions:

-After copying to inventory, find folder called 'Portal Gun [version number]'.

-Right click object 'Portal Gun' and select 'Wear'.

-After the Portal Gun attaches, click the 'Mouselook' button at the bottom of the screen.

-While in mouselook aim at an object and left mouse click to shoot the first portal, then aim at another object and left mouse again to shoot the second portal.

-Move towards portal A, and you will be pushed to portal B.

Keep in mind that the Portal Gun is still under development and may act unprictable on occasion..

Enjoy!
