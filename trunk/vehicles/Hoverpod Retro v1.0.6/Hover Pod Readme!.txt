To ride the Hover Pod:

1. Right-click the Hover Pod and select Ride from the menu.

2. Switch to Mouselook mode.

3. Press the arrow keys to drive and steer. 
    Press PgUp to jump. 
    Press PgDn to apply brakes.
    Click the Release Keys button on-screen to stop riding.
   
Tip: It's easiest to drive using the numeric keypad with Numlock turned off.
