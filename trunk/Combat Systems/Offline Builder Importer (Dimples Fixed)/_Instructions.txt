1. In Blender, click the Save button at the bottom of the editing window.
2. Save the file to a location you can easily get to (like the Desktop)
3. Open Notepad (or your favorite plain text editor), and open the file you just saved.
4. Select all the text in the file (Edit > Select All, or windows: ctrl+A, macintosh: command+A)
5. Copy the text to the clipboard (Edit > Copy, or windows: ctrl+C, macintosh: command+C)
6. Create a new notecard in your inventory, open it to edit.
7. Paste from the clipboard (Edit > Paste, or windows: ctrl+V, macintosh control+V)
8. Save the notecard.
9. Drag the notecard you just created onto the Offline Builder Importer
10. Wait. Your prims will rez shortly :-)
